Announcing Winners of Anna Devo Bhava–Food for All 2.0 Quiz

social share
15 Jun 2022

On the occasion of “International Women’s Day” being celebrated on 8 March 2022, “Anna Devo Bhava–Food for All 2.0 Quiz” was organized by Ministry of Food Processing Industries, Government of India during the month of March, 2022, in collaboration with MyGov team to create awareness among youth and masses about different aspects of food such as – food for marginalized, food as a purifier, food as spirituality, food for the healthy etc.

Anna Devo Bhava–Food for All 2.0 Quiz was live on the MyGov portal from 11th March, 2022 to 31st March, 2022. A total of 11,090 participants from all over the country took part in the quiz competition. The spirit and love of the participants for the significance of the Anna Devo Bhava–Food for All 2.0 Quiz is gratefully acknowledged. As per the guidelines of the quiz, winners have been finalized and distribution of e-certificates to winners is under process.
