The Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Anna Yojana (PM-GKAY) is an initiative under the Ministry of Consumer Affairs, Food and Public Distribution that intends to reduce the challenges encountered by the poor as a result of disruptions due to the COVID-19 pandemic.
